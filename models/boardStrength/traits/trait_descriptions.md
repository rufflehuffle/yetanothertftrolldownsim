# TFT Set 16 Trait Descriptions

---

## Origins

### Ascendant
After each combat, an Ascendant Charm appears in your shop. These are powerful effects that can be bought for gold.

**Tags:** noncombat

---

### Assimilator
Kai'Sa has a different Ability depending if her Attack Damage or Ability Power is higher.

**Tags:** flavor

---

### Bilgewater
Each player combat, gain Silver Serpents, plus 1 additional for each Bilgewater takedown. Silver Serpents can be spent in the Black Market to grant Bilgewater champions bonus stats and special loot. Higher tiers unlock rarer loot.

| Count | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| (3) | 15 Serpents |
| (5) | 35 Serpents, improved loot |
| (7) | 65 Serpents, superior loot |
| (10) | 150 Serpents, 2x stats, FIRE CANNONS! |

**Tags:**
(3): noncombat
(5): noncombat
(7): noncombat
(10): noncombat

### Blacksmith
After each player combat your strongest Ornn participates in, he makes progress forging an Artifact item. Each item he is equipped with grants him 350/600/2000 max Health. Artifacts grant 300% more.

**Tags:** noncombat tank-selfish

---

### Caretaker
After winning player combat, gain a random unit from Runeterra. After losing combat, gain free rerolls, with the amount increasing by stage.

**Tags:** noncombat

---

### Chainbreaker
Sylas rotates between 3 different Abilities, depending on which one is most useful at the moment. He cannot cast the same one twice in a row.

**Tags:** flavor

---

### Chronokeeper
Every 2 casts, the Chronokeeper stores 1 XP. If he has enough stored for you to level up, he transfers you the required XP. If you are level 10, he instead gains 15% Damage Amp.

**Tags:** noncombat dps-selfish

---

### Dark Child
Annie summons Tibbers on your bench. He can be fielded, takes up a team slot, and can be equipped with items separately. His star level is the same as Annie's.

**Tags:** flavor

### Glutton
Once per Planning phase, you can feed Tahm Kench a champion, permanently granting him either Health, Attack Speed, or Ability Power based on their role and star level.

**Tags:** flavor tank-selfish dps-selfish

---

### Harvester
Each time an enemy champion dies, gain 10 Mana. Casting abilities does not consume Mana. All enemies lose 10 Armor and Magic Resist.

**Tags:** dps-selfish dps-flat

---

### Heroic
Galio cannot be fielded in combat. Instead, his traits count from the bench and when Demacians Rally, he joins the fight. On landing, he creates a 3-hex shockwave that deals 10% of his max Health as magic damage. Enemies hit are knocked up for 1 second, reduced by distance from the center.

**Tags:** flavor dps-flat

---

### HexMech
Gain a Pilot Hex. Combat Start: the unit within jumps into T-Hex, granting her 80% of their Health and bonus stats based on their role and star level.

| Role | Bonus |
|------|-------|
| Tank | +20/30/40% Omnivamp |
| Fighter | +20/30/40% Attack Damage |
| Marksman | +12/20/30% Damage Amp |
| Caster | +2/4/5 Mana Regen |
| Assassin | +40/70/100% Crit Chance |

On death, the pilot ejects while T-Hex knocks back and Stuns nearby enemies for 1.5 seconds.

**Tags:** flavor tank-selfish dps-selfish

---

### Huntress
While your strongest Neeko is alive, your strongest Nidalee cannot be targeted by basic attacks and gains 50% Durability.

**Tags:** flavor tank-selfish

### Immortal

**Tags:** flavor tank-selfish

### Riftscourge
Baron Nashor takes up 2 team slots and grants +2 to the Void trait. After 8 seconds, the board enters a Void Rift. The Rift grants Void units 30% Damage Amp and constantly strikes enemies with bolts of plasma, dealing 5% max Health as magic damage.

### Rune Mage

### Soulbound
Lucian and Senna fight together, swapping when either casts. Each champion has a unique ability. Your strongest Soulbound pair grants bonuses to your team while they survive.
- While Senna is out: all allies gain 7% Damage Reduction.
- While Lucian is out: all allies gain 7% Damage Amp.

**Tags:** flavor tank-splash dps-splash

### The Boss
When Sett first drops below 45% max Health, he leaves combat to start doing sit-ups. Each sit-up restores 15% max Health and gives him 60% Attack and Move Speed. If he reaches full Health, he returns to combat Pumped Up, converting all damage from his attacks and Ability to true damage. If all of his allies die, he immediately returns to combat.

**Tags:** flavor tank-selfish dps-selfish
